THRILLING SPORTS
Our adventure and trekking camps are designed for aged 18 to 35 years, who are looking for a chance to challenge themselves and explore the beauty of nature. We understand that every student is unique and has their own interests, which is why we have a variety of adventures to choose from. From panoramic views of snow-capped mountains to high-adrenaline white-water rafting, we have something for everyone. Our popular adventures include the Chandrashila Chopta Trek, Manali Snow Trails, Dandeli Adventure Camp, and Rishikesh Multi Activity Camp. We also offer closer destinations like Panchgani Adventure and Vasota Voyagers for those who prefer to stay closer to Pune.
